Privacy Policy — Vitty.ai

Effective date: 27 Aug 2025

App: Vitty.ai

Vitty.ai is a personal-finance assistant. We respect your privacy and collect only what's needed to deliver core features like secure login, chat, speech-to-text, and document/image uploads.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account Data: phone number or email (e.g., Firebase/Google Sign-In), display name, and basic profile details you provide.
  • Content You Provide: chat messages, documents/images you upload, and preferences/goals you set.
  • Usage & Device Data: app events, device/OS info, crash logs, diagnostics (to maintain reliability).
  • Microphone Audio (with consent): short snippets strictly to convert speech to text when you tap the mic.
  • Camera/Photos (with consent): images/documents you choose for upload or scan.
We do not sell your personal data.

2) Why We Use Your Data

  • Core features: chat, speech-to-text, document/image upload & analysis, personalized insights.
  • Security & Account: authentication, fraud prevention, and keeping your account safe.
  • Improvement: debugging, analytics, performance, and feature enhancements.
  • Compliance: meeting legal and regulatory requirements.

3) Permissions We Request & How We Use Them

We ask for permissions only when needed and only while you use the related feature. You can deny or revoke any permission in device settings (some features may stop working).

android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO

Used only when you tap the mic for speech-to-text. Audio is processed on-device and/or securely on our servers/authorized processors solely to produce text. No background recording.

android.permission.CAMERA

Used to scan/capture documents or images that you explicitly choose to upload in chat. No continuous camera access.

android.permission.RECEIVE_SMS, android.permission.READ_SMS

Used only to auto-detect OTP for phone verification, so you don't have to type it manually. We do not read or store other SMS content.

Note: If you prefer, you can enter OTP manually and skip SMS permissions.

android.permission.USE_BIOMETRIC

Lets you unlock the app with device biometrics (Face/Touch). Biometrics are processed by Android's secure hardware/OS. We never receive or store your raw biometric data.

android.permission.INTERNET

Required for secure communication with our backend (authentication, chat, speech processing, document analysis, and updates).

android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (legacy devices only)

Used when you select files/images to upload or export on older Android versions. We access only the items you choose—not your entire storage. On newer Android versions we use scoped storage/system pickers.

android.permission.MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (only if explicitly enabled)

If enabled, it supports advanced file actions (e.g., exporting user-requested files). We do not scan or collect unrelated files. If you don't need this, you can keep it disabled; we recommend using scoped storage.

4) Where We Process & Store Data

We use secure cloud infrastructure (e.g., Google Firebase for auth/analytics; managed servers for chat and processing). Data may be processed in INDIA to ensure reliability and performance.

5) Sharing & Third Parties

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the app (e.g., Firebase Auth/Analytics, secure storage, speech-to-text processors) under strict contracts that limit use to our instructions.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security. We do not sell your personal information.

6) Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

  • OTP SMS content is used transiently for verification and not retained.
  • Microphone audio is kept only as long as needed to produce text.
  • You can request deletion of your account/data (see below).

7) Security

We employ industry-standard safeguards: encryption in transit, restricted access, monitoring, and secure key management. No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

8) Your Choices & Rights

  • Permissions: manage Microphone/Camera/SMS/Storage permissions in your device settings.
  • Access & Update: view/change your profile in the app.
  • Delete: request account/data deletion via in-app support or email.
  • Analytics controls: where available, you can opt out of certain analytics; otherwise contact us.

9) Children

Vitty.ai is not directed to children below the applicable age of consent (e.g., 13/16). We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate consent.

10) International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than yours. We apply safeguards consistent with applicable laws.

11) Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted in-app or by updating the "Effective date".
